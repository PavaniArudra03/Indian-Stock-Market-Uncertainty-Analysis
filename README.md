# Investors' Response to Economic Uncertainty: An Analysis of the Indian Stock Market

## Overview

This project examines how investors respond to periods of economic uncertainty in the Indian stock market. The study analyzes investor behavior, market trends, and factors influencing investment decisions using Excel-based data analysis.

## Objectives

* To identify how investors change their investment behavior during periods of economic uncertainty.
* To examine how economic uncertainty affects overall market trends in the Indian stock market.
* To analyze which sectors of the Indian stock market are most sensitive and responsive to uncertain economic conditions.
